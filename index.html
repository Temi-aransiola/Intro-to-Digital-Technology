Outline of the Grade 10 computer Science course

By Temi.

The Grade 10 computer technology course introduced me and my fellow class mates to computer systems, breaking down and building back computers, as well as understanding electricity and coding. There were 6 main units in this course these include:

1 Introduction

2 Electricity

3 Bits and Bytes

4 tinkercad project

5 CSE days

6 Computer work
